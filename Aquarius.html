ABOUT AQUARIUS

Best Time to View

Aquarius appears in the night sky almost all-year long from April to January.

  • In the Northern Hemisphere, the best time to view this constellation is during fall.
  • In the Southern Hemisphere, the best time to view this constellation is during spring.

    • Myth

    In Greek mythology, Aquarius was Ganymede. Ganymede is a young boy who was kidnapped by Aquila, Zeus' eagle, to be the cupbearer for the Olympian gods. The constellation, Crater, is often thought to be Ganymede's cup.
